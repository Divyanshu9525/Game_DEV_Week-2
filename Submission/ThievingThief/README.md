## Thieving Thief

#Controls
    -WASD for movement and mouse for panning and left control for crouch
    -In scene1 mouse pan is availabe -45 to 45 degree but in scene2 its 360 movable as the map is hard to see through with my camera setup

#Features
    -Crouch with left control
    -Used Physics.SphereCast to simulate proper cone
    -Enemies remember position for 2 seconds (might jump you if you come out of the safe zone too soon)
    -Total credits is updated in ui(pop up not enabled)
    -Music (pretty good if I must say)
